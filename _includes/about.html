About

The highly acclaimed Martinez family, whose roots are in Northern New Mexico, provide an unparalleled musical experience. The history behind the music and the history behind the musicians themselves are legendary.
Los Martinez would like to share the music, the history and the legends with you.
Los Martinez are brothers who share a legacy of traditional Southwestern music which dates to the earliest settlement of Northern New Mexico. Lorenzo Martinez is recognized by the Smithsonian Institution as a master folk violinist. Lorenzo is an National Endowment for the Arts Heritage Fellow, having received the prestigious award in 2003 for preserving Traditional New Mexican Hispanic Folk Music. Rob Martinez, guitarist and vocalist and historian is also a highly sought after genealogist, lecturer of Southwestern History and published author.
Los Martinez will take you on a musical journey which begins with Traditional New Mexico Violin and Guitar music, delights you with regional Southwestern Music from the twentieth century, serenades and excites you with Mariachi and astounds you with the unexpected.
